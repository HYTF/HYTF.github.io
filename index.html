A Special Chinese History and Culture Class

HYTF is an organization of Chinese history and culture lecture proposed by parents and organized independently by children.
In order to improve the students' speech and learning ability, each member will share their Chinese history and culture in order to enrich studentsâ€™ knowledge and understanding of Chinese culture.
