About CHHC

Objective

In order to improve the students' speech and learning ability, each member will share their Chinese history and culture in order to enrich students' knowledge and understanding of Chinese culture. Through the exchange platform of CHHC, more and more people will experience the charm of Chinese cultures and learn them easily through communication and sharing in the future. The development of the Chinese history and culture lecture such that this is conducive for students to become initiative learning. This better allows students to absorb and learn the history knowledge on their own which they previously considered tedious. In addition, members in CHHC will share courseware content with other members in a simple and easy-to-understand manner in order to promote them to develop good habits of preparation before class, self-learning, and language organization.

Lecture Form

CHHC has continued to carry out lectures focused on lecture abilitySince the beginning of the activity. Each member will present a presentation (Powerpoint/Google Slides) and give the audience their own speeches (view sharing).

Recent Results

Members have gradually evolved from obscure to fluency and expressive. The courseware content was simple and overly depending on web resources. It is rich in content now and can be easily understood using their own language and the mainline is clear. The progress of the members occurred as the activities proceeded. It is not only through the members' own efforts but also self-improvement in conjunction with the speech experiences and feedback of other members.

Future Plan

The plan for the future of the CHHC is to introduce excellent courseware and do outside Chinese cultural exchanges and lectures.
