About HYTF

Objective

In order to improve the students' speech and learning ability, each member will share their Chinese history and culture in order to enrich students' knowledge and understanding of Chinese culture. Through the exchange platform of HYTF, more and more people will experience the charm of Chinese cultures and learn them easily through communication and sharing in the future. The idea is that the development of the Chinese history and culture lecture such that this is conducive for students to become initiative learning. This better allows students to absorb and learn the history knowledge on their own which they previously considered tedious. In addition, members in HYTF will share courseware content with other members in a simple and easy-to-understand manner in order to promote them to develop good habits of preparation before class, self-learning, and language organization.

Lecture Form

HYTF has continued to carry out lectures focused on lecture ability. Since the beginning of the activity. Each member will present a presentation (Powerpoint/Google Slides) and give the audience their own speeches (view sharing).

Members

The age range of members of HYTF is from 9 to 16 years old. There are currently 10 members in this organization. Members' names: Linda Liu, Tony Liao, Leon Yang, Nancy Liang, Jason Yang, Tony Liang, Sonia Qi, Cassie Chen, Sophia Liu.

Recent Results

Members have gradually evolved from obscure to fluency and expressive. The courseware content was simple and overly depending on web resources. It is rich in content now and can be easily understood using their own language and the mainline is clear. The progress of the members occurred as the activities proceeded. It is not only through the members' own efforts but also self-improvement in conjunction with the speech experiences and feedback of other members.

Future Plan

The plan for the future of the HYTF is to introduce excellent courseware and do outside Chinese cultural exchanges and lectures. To better communicate people from different places around the world who would like to speak Chinese and learn Chinese history and culture, we established an activity called Chinese Corner.
